Last Updated: July 27, 2025

Your privacy is important to me. This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and protect your information when you use my website.

Information I Collect

This website does not have a contact form or account system that stores your information. The only way to contact me is by using a direct email link (e.g., clicking an “Email Me” button), which opens your own email client. When you email me directly, your email address and message are handled entirely through your chosen email service and my email inbox.

How I Use Your Information

If you choose to send me an email, I will only use the information you provide (such as your name, email address, and message) to respond to your inquiry. I do not share, sell, or use your information for marketing purposes.

How Your Information Is Stored

Because this site does not store form submissions, no personal information is stored on the website itself. Any information you send by email remains in my email account and is protected by the security measures of my email provider. Please avoid sending sensitive personal information in your email.

Third‑Party Services

This website does not send your data to any third‑party services. Standard web hosting logs (such as basic analytics, server logs, or error logs) may be collected by the hosting provider, but I do not personally collect or process them.

Your Rights

You may request that I delete or correct any personal information I hold about you, except where I am required to keep certain information for legal, tax, or legitimate business record‑keeping purposes. .

Changes to This Policy
